Centralized Patient Record Management System
--------------------------------------------

A comprehensive Django-based healthcare platform
for securely managing and sharing patient health data
using Aadhaar verification (via OTP or QR code).

Developed as a Major Project for Academic/Research purposes.

Core Modules:
- accounts: Aadhaar verification and authentication
- patients: Patient record uploads and grouping
- hospitals: (future) hospital-side request management
- core: project-level configuration, homepage, and routing
